# HW2 - Charles McMillan

## Workflow
The workflow selected for this project is Customer Success email drafting. This involves generating professional, context-aware email responses to customers based on specific scenarios such as product inquiries, support issues, renewal discussions, or customer concerns.

## User
The primary user is a Customer Success Manager (CSM) responsible for managing client relationships, responding to customer inquiries, and ensuring customer satisfaction and retention.

## Input
The system receives:
- A customer scenario or message (e.g., inquiry, complaint, renewal concern)
- Optional context such as:
  - Tone (e.g., empathetic, professional, urgent)
  - Internal notes or background information

## Output
The system produces:
- A polished, professional email draft that:
  - Clearly addresses the customer's request or concern
  - Maintains appropriate tone and professionalism
  - Avoids making unsupported claims or promises
  - Is ready for review, editing, and sending by the user

## Business Value
This task is valuable to automate or partially automate because:
- **Time efficiency:** Customer Success Managers spend significant time drafting repetitive emails
- **Consistency:** Ensures uniform tone, messaging, and quality across communications
- **Scalability:** Enables handling higher volumes of customer interactions without sacrificing quality
- **Risk reduction:** Provides structured drafts that can reduce miscommunication when reviewed before sending
- **Focus on high-value work:** Allows CSMs to focus more on strategy, relationship building, and complex problem-solving rather than routine writing tasks
